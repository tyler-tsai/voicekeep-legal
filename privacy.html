Voicekeep — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-23

Voicekeep is a voice-driven personal expense tracker. This policy describes what data the app processes, where it goes, and how long it stays. We collect the minimum needed to make the voice loop work; nothing else.

1. Data the app processes

Voice recordings. When you tap record, the app captures a short audio clip (≤ 10 seconds). The clip is sent over HTTPS to our proxy (voice-keys-proxy.tyler90177.workers.dev), which forwards it to OpenAI / OpenRouter for transcription and natural-language parsing. The clip is held in memory only — it is not written to disk on the proxy and is not stored by us. OpenAI's and OpenRouter's retention policies apply to the brief interval the clip exists on their servers; see their respective privacy notices.

Transcripts and parsed transactions. The text returned from the voice loop is saved on your device in Apple's SwiftData store. This data never leaves your phone unless you explicitly export it (CSV) or share it.

Device attestation key. On first launch the app generates a unique cryptographic key in your iPhone's Secure Enclave and registers the public half with our proxy (via Apple's App Attest). The private half never leaves your device. This key prevents abuse of our proxy — without it the daily request cap can't be enforced fairly.

Subscription state. If you purchase Voicekeep Plus, StoreKit hands the app an Apple-signed receipt (JWS) which we forward to our proxy. The proxy records only:

  • Your device attestation key id (a hash, not personally identifying)
  • The product id (monthly vs annual)
  • The subscription expiry timestamp
  • Apple's original-transaction id (for refund correlation)

We do not receive your Apple ID, name, email, payment card, or billing address. Apple handles all payment.

2. Data we do NOT collect

  • No analytics SDK (Firebase, Mixpanel, Sentry, etc.)
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No location data
  • No contacts, photos, or microphone access outside of explicit recording
  • No name, email, or account login (the app has no user accounts)

3. AI Observations (opt-in)

If you enable AI Observations in Settings, the app sends an aggregated monthly summary of your transactions (categories + sums, no merchant names unless you allow them) to OpenAI for natural-language insight generation. The summary is generated on-demand and discarded after the response returns. You can disable this feature at any time in Settings.

4. Where data lives

  • On your iPhone: all transactions, budgets, categories, recurring rules, mochi state. Backed up via iCloud iff you have iCloud Backup enabled at the OS level (Voicekeep does not use iCloud sync directly).
  • On our proxy (Cloudflare Workers KV): device attestation public key + subscription tier record. Both are keyed by an opaque hash, not by anything that identifies you personally.
  • On OpenAI / OpenRouter: only the in-flight voice clip during the seconds it takes to transcribe. Subject to their retention.

5. Retention

  • On-device data: lives until you delete the app.
  • Proxy device records: kept indefinitely while you use the app; deleted on request (email below).
  • Subscription records: kept indefinitely for support / refund handling.

6. Children

Voicekeep is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

7. Your rights

You can:

  • Delete all on-device data by uninstalling the app.
  • Cancel your subscription anytime via iOS Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions.
  • Request deletion of your proxy-side records by emailing the address below; we'll confirm within 30 days.

8. Changes to this policy

If we change this policy materially we'll update the "Last updated" date and post the change here. For non-trivial changes affecting how data is used, we'll surface the change in-app on next launch.
