Tonecast — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-23

Tonecast is a personal voice keyboard for iOS — it adds a system-wide keyboard that lets you dictate, shift tone, and translate between Chinese and English. This policy describes what data the app and the keyboard extension process, where it goes, and how long it stays. We collect the minimum needed to make the keyboard work; nothing else.

1. Data the app processes

Voice recordings. When you tap the mic in the Tonecast keyboard or in the host app, the app captures a short audio clip (≤ 60 seconds). The clip is sent over HTTPS to our proxy (voice-keys-proxy.tyler90177.workers.dev), which forwards it to OpenAI / OpenRouter for transcription and tone rewriting. The clip is held in memory only — it is not written to disk on the proxy and is not stored by us. OpenAI's and OpenRouter's retention policies apply to the brief interval the clip exists on their servers; see their respective privacy notices.

Transcripts and rewrites. The text returned from the voice loop is inserted into whichever app you're typing into. Recent transcripts are temporarily cached in App Group storage on your device so the keyboard's "Recents" panel can show them — they never leave your phone and are pruned automatically.

Device attestation key. On first launch the app generates a unique cryptographic key in your iPhone's Secure Enclave and registers the public half with our proxy (via Apple's App Attest). The private half never leaves your device. This key prevents abuse of our proxy — without it the daily request cap can't be enforced fairly.

Subscription state. If you purchase Tonecast Plus, StoreKit hands the app an Apple-signed receipt (JWS) which we forward to our proxy. The proxy records only:

  • Your device attestation key id (a hash, not personally identifying)
  • The product id (monthly vs annual)
  • The subscription expiry timestamp
  • Apple's original-transaction id (for refund correlation)

We do not receive your Apple ID, name, email, payment card, or billing address. Apple handles all payment.

2. Keyboard "Full Access" permission

The Tonecast keyboard requires Full Access (iOS Settings → General → Keyboard → Keyboards → Tonecast → Allow Full Access). This is required so the keyboard extension can:

  • Reach our proxy over HTTPS for transcription and tone rewriting (iOS denies all network access to keyboards without Full Access).
  • Share state with the main Tonecast app via App Group storage (settings, tone style guides, Flow Session state).

Tonecast does not read or store the text you type into other apps. The keyboard only transmits audio you explicitly record by tapping the mic. We do not log keystrokes, passwords, or unrelated input.

3. Data we do NOT collect

  • No analytics SDK (Firebase, Mixpanel, Sentry, etc.)
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No location data
  • No contacts, photos, or microphone access outside of explicit recording
  • No keystroke logging — the keyboard only sends audio you record
  • No name, email, or account login (the app has no user accounts)

4. Where data lives

  • On your iPhone: tone style guides, personal vocabulary, default tone / translate preferences, Flow Session state, recent transcripts. Backed up via iCloud iff you have iCloud Backup enabled at the OS level (Tonecast does not use iCloud sync directly).
  • On our proxy (Cloudflare Workers KV): device attestation public key + subscription tier record. Both are keyed by an opaque hash, not by anything that identifies you personally.
  • On OpenAI / OpenRouter: only the in-flight voice clip during the seconds it takes to transcribe + the rewrite text. Subject to their retention.

5. Retention

  • On-device data: lives until you delete the app.
  • Proxy device records: kept indefinitely while you use the app; deleted on request (email below).
  • Subscription records: kept indefinitely for support / refund handling.

6. Children

Tonecast is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

7. Your rights

You can:

  • Delete all on-device data by uninstalling the app.
  • Revoke the keyboard's Full Access in iOS Settings → General → Keyboard → Tonecast.
  • Cancel your subscription anytime via iOS Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions.
  • Request deletion of your proxy-side records by emailing the address below; we'll confirm within 30 days.

8. Changes to this policy

If we change this policy materially we'll update the "Last updated" date and post the change here. For non-trivial changes affecting how data is used, we'll surface the change in-app on next launch.
