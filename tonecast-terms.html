Tonecast is a personal voice keyboard that transcribes speech and rewrites it in different tones, including Chinese ⇄ English translation. The Free tier allows up to 200 backend requests per day. Tonecast Plus lifts the cap to 1,000/day. Both tiers depend on third-party APIs (OpenAI, OpenRouter, Cloudflare) — if those services are unavailable, dictation may be temporarily degraded.

2. Keyboard Full Access

The Tonecast keyboard requires Full Access to make network requests and share state with the main app. Tonecast does not log keystrokes or read text in other apps; the keyboard only transmits audio you explicitly record by tapping the mic. See the Privacy Policy for details.
